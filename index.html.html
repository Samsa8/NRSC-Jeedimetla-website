NRSC Outreach Facility, Hyderabad

NRSC Students Projects/ Internship Academic Collabrations
This facility plays a pivotal role in mentoring the students, knowledge transfer and capacity building within the realm of Earth Observation (EO) and geo-spatial technologies.

Student Projects:

Eligibility: Open to BE/BTech/MCA/ME/MTech and BSc/Diploma/MSc/PhD students or recent graduates within six months with a minimum 60% aggregate or CGPA of 6.32.

Duration: Minimum 3 months.

Fields: Projects are offered in the fields of RS&GIS, Applications, Geo-informatics, Engineering branches, etc.

Details: Considering the interest of the student and the organization, areas and guides are decided. Students contribute in all areas of NRSC, encouraged to work in ongoing projects using high-resolution spatial data. Offline mode of working.

About NRSC

Our Activities: National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC) is one of the primary centres of Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), Department of Space (DOS). NRSC has the mandate for establishment of ground stations for receiving satellite data, generation of data products, dissemination to the users, development of techniques for remote sensing applications including disaster management support, geospatial services for good governance, and capacity building for professionals, faculty, and students.
  • Main Campus at Balanagar, Hyderabad for Administration, Remote Sensing Applications, and Aerial Services
  • Campus at Shadnagar for Satellite Data Reception, Data Processing and Dissemination, Earth and Climate Studies, and Disaster Management Support
  • Five Regional Centres:
    1. Sector 9, KBHB in Jodhpur (Regional Centre-West)
    2. Sadhiknagar at New Delhi (Regional Centre-North)
    3. New Salt Lake City in Kolkata (Regional Centre-East)
    4. Amaravathy Road in Nagpur (Regional Centre-Central)
    5. Karthik Nagar in Bangalore (Regional Centre-South)
  • Aircraft operations facility at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad
  • Training Education and Outreach Group facility at Jeedimetla in Hyderabad for providing training for professionals, faculty, and students and for general outreach.
  • Group Director of TEOG is Shri M.A Fyzee

Outreach

The National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC), a key division of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), operates a dedicated Outreach Facility in the Jeedimetla neighborhood of Hyderabad. This facility works towards spreading awareness about ISRO programme and Space Research among the students and public. It deals with Industrial Visits, Instation and Outstation programmes.

Training

The National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC), headquartered in Hyderabad and part of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), provides comprehensive training programs in Jeedimetla focused on remote sensing, geospatial technologies, and their real-world applications. These hands-on programs cater to students, researchers, professionals, and government personnel.
